ANKIT

- THE UNTOLD STORY -

Ek ladka... jo hasta tha sabke saamne,
par andar hi andar har din toot raha tha.

"Chehre pe muskaan thi, par dil mein andhera tha...
Jo sabko apna maanta tha, uska apna koi na tha."
🎧 STAY STRONG 👑 KEEP PUSHING ⚡

Phase 1 - Class 10th

15 saal ka Ankit...
Jab dusre bachche sirf padh rahe the,
tab woh expectations aur pressure ke neeche dab raha tha...
Family... studies... aur comparisons...

"Kitabon ke bojh ke neeche... ek bachpan chup sa gaya..."

Phase 2 - Silence

Woh bolna chahta tha...
Par sunne wala koi nahi tha...
Dheere dheere usne khamoshi ko apna liya...

"Jab alfaaz saath chhod dete hain... tab khamoshi sab keh deti hai..."

Phase 3 - Lost

Kabhi kabhi sab kuch itna bhaari ho jaata hai...
Ki insaan bas khud ko dhoondhne nikal padta hai...

"Bhaagna kamzori nahi... kabhi kabhi bachna hota hai..."

Phase 4 - Return

Wapas toh aa gaya...
Par jo andar toot gaya tha...
Woh waise ka waisa hi reh gaya...

"Sabne chehra dekha... kisi ne dard nahi..."

Phase 5 - Raushan

Ek bhai tha... Raushan...
Zyada bolta nahi tha...
Par har baar saath khada tha...
Kabhi kabhi bas itna hi kaafi hota hai...

"Kuch rishte awaaz nahi karte... bas sambhal lete hain..."

Phase 6 - Still Fighting

Aaj bhi ladai chal rahi hai...
Mind se... pressure se... khud se...
Par Ankit ab bhi khada hai...

"Main टूटा zaroor hoon... par khatam nahi."

Final Words

Ye sirf ek story nahi...
Har us insaan ki kahani hai...
Jo chup rehkar bhi roz lad raha hai...

"Kuch dard dikhte nahi... sirf mehsoos hote hain..."
